Meet The Team

Detailed attention from experienced black belt instructors.

Senior Instructor

Sensei Mikey

Sensei Mikey started his Ju-Jitsu journey in 2011, and was assisting in classes within a year. Acheiving his black belt in 2014, he has worked in both clubs and communities teaching martial arts and self defence, as well as leading many programs in schools, training students between the ages of primary one and sixth form. He is the head coach for The Dojo (WJJFI headquarters) Juniors. He has been operating as head coach and growing multiple clubs since 2021, with the belief that everyone deserves the chance to train and become a better version of themselves.
